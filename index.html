RECIPE CARD

HUMMUS

Serve 3 Adult

Ingredient

  • 200g/ 7
  • 2 teaspoon of leamon joice
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • Salt and peper to taste
  • Add sugarcane water
  • add orange
  • Add grandnut oil

Method

  1. Put the chickpeas, lemon choice, tarlin, garlic, salat and pepper in a blender and blend
  2. Add a little of the water to make a paste shift
  3. The amont of water you need we vary so add a little at a time
  4. you can add alittle more salt and pepper lemon juice to taste
  5. You can also add some alive salt for extra richness
